About HW STUDIOS

HW STUDIOS由张智宸和王雍成立的游戏工作室，自2025年成立以来，我们始终致力于打造令人难忘的游戏体验。由游戏开发者同学和编程爱好者同学组成的团队，融合前沿技术与艺术表达，持续为玩家带来惊喜。

HW STUDIOS is a game studio founded by Zhang Zhichen and Wang Yong. Since its establishment in 2025, we have been committed to creating unforgettable gaming experiences. Our team, composed of student game developers and programming enthusiasts, combines cutting-edge technology with artistic expression to continuously surprise players.

我们相信游戏是连接世界的桥梁，在追求卓越品质的同时，始终坚持「玩家至上」的开发理念。

We believe games are a bridge connecting the world. While pursuing excellent quality, we always adhere to the "player-first" development philosophy.
